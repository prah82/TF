10+ Questions Answered

What products does Tideforge Global Trading Pvt Ltd supply?

We supply a wide range of high-quality commodities including agricultural produce, metals and minerals, energy products and industrial raw materials.

Which countries do you serve?

We have an active presence across more than 50 countries, supported by a strong network of partners and shipping lines.

How can I request a quote?

Visit our Contact page or email us with your product, quantity and destination. Our trade team typically responds within 24 hours.

Do you handle small as well as bulk orders?

Yes. We specialise in bulk supply but accommodate trial and mid-volume orders for new clients.

What payment terms do you offer?

We accept Letters of Credit (LC), TT, escrow and other internationally accepted payment methods, agreed per contract.

Can you assist with trade finance?

Yes. We work with reputed banks and partners to support LC issuance, discounting and structured finance.

Are prices quoted FOB or CIF?

Both. Quotations can be on FOB, CFR or CIF basis as per Incoterms 2020.

How are shipments delivered?

By sea, air or multimodal transport, with our team handling documentation, customs clearance and last-mile coordination.

How is product quality verified?

Every consignment is subject to independent third-party inspection (SGS, Bureau Veritas or equivalent) with full compliance documentation.

What are typical lead times?

Most orders are dispatched within 2–4 weeks of contract confirmation. Urgent requirements can be accommodated.
